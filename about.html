About

Everything affecting your day in Neepawa, on one page.

CityPulse pulls live data from official public feeds — weather, severe-weather alerts, highway conditions, closures, and cameras — and refreshes itself around the clock. There is no human in the loop.

What updates automatically

  • Current weather & 4-day forecast — Open-Meteo, for Neepawa's exact coordinates. Refreshed every 30 minutes.
  • Severe weather alerts — Environment Canada (Brandon forecast region, the closest coverage to Neepawa). Checked every 15 minutes; official alerts appear as a red banner.
  • Road closures & traffic events — Manitoba 511, filtered to within 60 km of Neepawa. Checked every 15 minutes.
  • Highway driving conditions — Manitoba 511 winter road condition reports for highways around Neepawa (PTH 16, PTH 5, Hwy 1 and area). Checked every 30 minutes; mainly published in winter.
  • Highway cameras — the nearest Manitoba 511 cameras, with images that refresh while you have the page open.

Data caveats

  • Environment Canada alerts come from the Brandon forecast region — Neepawa shares almost all severe-weather alerts with it, but very localized warnings may not appear.
  • Weather values are model data interpolated to Neepawa's coordinates; the nearest official observation station is Brandon Airport.
  • Road conditions are seasonal — Manitoba 511 reports them mainly from fall to spring.

What this is not

⚠ Not an emergency service

If you see downed power lines, a fire, a gas leak, or anyone hurt — call 911. CityPulse is informational only and should never delay an emergency call.

How it's built

Static HTML/CSS/JS hosted on GitHub Pages. A scheduled GitHub Actions workflow polls the public feeds every 20 minutes and republishes the site with fresh data. There is no server and no database — which also means there is nothing to hack, nothing to overload, and nothing that costs money to run. The Manitoba 511 API key lives in an encrypted GitHub secret and never reaches the browser.
