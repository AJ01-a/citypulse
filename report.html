Reporting tips

  • Be specific. "Power out, 4 houses on Elm St" is more useful than "outage."
  • Drop a pin. Maps help others identify affected areas quickly.
  • Don't report emergencies here. Call 911 for fires, downed wires, or injuries.
  • Update later. If service returns, you can mark your report resolved from the dashboard.

Reports are anonymous

No accounts, no tracking. Reports are stored locally in your browser and shared with the community feed.
